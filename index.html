Featured Project

Loyal

2020-2021

This was a project that I worked on at The Engine Is Red. Loyal is a company that develops drugs that help large-breed dogs live longer, healthier lives. With creative direction from Mitch Kelly, art direction from Mara Larson, and development from Daryl Knight, we were able to create a brand identity and a website for Loyal. It was a fun challenge for me to balance the highy scientific nature of this project with a fun, approachable aesthetic and voice (spoiler alert - there are lots of doggo illustrations).
